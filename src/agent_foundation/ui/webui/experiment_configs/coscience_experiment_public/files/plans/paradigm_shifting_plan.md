## 🎯 RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
Explore innovative approaches that could significantly advance the field. Aim for novel contributions that push beyond current limitations.

## 📋 EVOLVE METHODOLOGY

### Phase 1: Codebase Investigation
- Analyze provided codebases to understand architecture, patterns, and data flows
- Document key modules, interfaces, and dependencies
- Identify fundamental limitations and potential improvement areas
- Generate comprehensive documentation for reference

### Phase 2: Research Planning
Apply systematic reasoning patterns to generate targeted research queries:
- **Barrier-First**: What fundamental limits exist that constrain current approaches?
- **Cross-Domain Transfer**: What promising solutions from other fields might apply?
- **Mathematical Equivalence**: What theoretical insights could enable new approaches?
- **Assumption Challenging**: What assumptions might be limiting progress?
- **Scalability Inversion**: How to make elegant approaches practical at scale?

### Phase 3: Deep Research
- Execute parallel research streams on top-ranked queries
- Survey cutting-edge advances from top-tier conferences and papers
- Synthesize findings into actionable insights with citations
- Focus on novel opportunities beyond incremental improvements

### Phase 4: Proposal Generation
- Generate concrete architectural proposals from research findings
- Include mathematical formulations and complexity analysis
- Specify expected impact and validation approach
- Target significant improvements over existing approaches

### Phase 5: Merge & Synthesize
- Combine individual proposals into unified architecture
- Resolve interfaces and design tradeoffs
- Create implementation roadmap with realistic milestones

### Phase 6: Implementation
- Implement proposed components with clean interfaces
- Follow existing codebase conventions
- Include documentation

### Phase 7: Experiments
- Validate on appropriate benchmark datasets
- Measure relevant metrics (accuracy, throughput, etc.)
- Compare against state-of-the-art baselines

### Phase 8: Analysis
- Analyze results and identify remaining opportunities
- Perform ablation studies to understand contributions
- Diagnose root causes for any gaps vs. targets
- **Determine if another iteration is needed for further improvements**

## 🔄 ITERATION POLICY
After analysis, if significant improvement opportunities remain:
- Focus next iteration on the highest-leverage bottleneck
- Apply targeted research to that specific area
- Validate that improvements are meaningful and well-grounded

## ✅ SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Achieve meaningful improvements over baselines
- Each phase produces documented artifacts
- Proposals are grounded in research evidence and novel insights
- Implementations validated experimentally with clear results
- Iterations justified by identified opportunities, not assumptions
