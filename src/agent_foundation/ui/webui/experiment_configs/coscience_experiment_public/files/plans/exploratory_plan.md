## 🎯 RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
Understand the landscape and identify potential opportunity areas. Focus on learning and discovery rather than immediate solutions.

## 📋 EVOLVE METHODOLOGY

### Phase 1: Codebase Investigation
- Analyze provided codebases to understand architecture, patterns, and data flows
- Document key modules, interfaces, and dependencies
- Map the design space and identify areas of interest
- Generate comprehensive documentation for future reference

### Phase 2: Research Planning
Apply exploratory reasoning patterns to generate discovery queries:
- **Landscape Mapping**: What are the major approaches in this space?
- **Trend Analysis**: What directions is the field moving toward?
- **Gap Identification**: What problems remain unsolved or under-explored?
- **Opportunity Scanning**: Where might novel contributions be possible?
- **Connection Finding**: What related fields might offer insights?

### Phase 3: Deep Research
- Execute broad research across the problem space
- Survey diverse approaches from multiple sources
- Synthesize findings into a comprehensive landscape view
- Focus on understanding rather than solving

### Phase 4: Proposal Generation
- Generate potential research directions from findings
- Outline possible approaches without committing to specifics
- Identify promising areas for deeper investigation
- Document trade-offs between different directions

### Phase 5: Merge & Synthesize
- Create a unified view of the opportunity landscape
- Prioritize areas by potential impact and feasibility
- Outline possible next steps for each direction

### Phase 6: Implementation
- Create proof-of-concept prototypes if appropriate
- Implement exploratory experiments
- Document findings and learnings

### Phase 7: Experiments
- Run exploratory experiments to validate assumptions
- Measure baseline metrics for future comparison
- Test initial hypotheses

### Phase 8: Analysis
- Synthesize learnings from exploration
- Identify most promising directions
- Document open questions and uncertainties
- **Recommend next steps for deeper investigation**

## 🔄 ITERATION POLICY
After analysis, based on exploration findings:
- May pivot to targeted or systematic approach if clear opportunity identified
- May continue exploration in promising sub-areas
- May conclude exploration if landscape is well-understood

## ✅ QUALITY CRITERIA
- Comprehensive landscape understanding achieved
- Major approaches documented with trade-offs
- Promising opportunities identified
- Open questions clearly articulated
- Foundation laid for future focused work
