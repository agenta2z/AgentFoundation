## 🎯 RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
Find meaningful improvements through structured analysis and experimentation. Focus on systematic gains that are practical and well-validated.

## 📋 EVOLVE METHODOLOGY

### Phase 1: Codebase Investigation
- Analyze provided codebases to understand architecture, patterns, and data flows
- Document key modules, interfaces, and dependencies
- Identify improvement opportunities and potential optimization areas
- Generate comprehensive documentation for reference

### Phase 2: Research Planning
Apply systematic reasoning patterns to generate targeted research queries:
- **Barrier-First**: What practical limits exist that constrain current performance?
- **Cross-Domain Transfer**: What proven solutions from other fields might apply?
- **Mathematical Equivalence**: What theoretical insights could improve efficiency?
- **Assumption Challenging**: What assumptions might be limiting performance?
- **Scalability Inversion**: How to improve efficiency without sacrificing quality?

### Phase 3: Deep Research
- Execute parallel research streams on top-ranked queries
- Survey recent advances from relevant conferences and papers
- Synthesize findings into actionable insights with citations
- Focus on practical improvements with clear implementation paths

### Phase 4: Proposal Generation
- Generate concrete improvement proposals from research findings
- Include mathematical formulations and complexity analysis
- Specify expected impact metrics and validation approach
- Target meaningful, measurable improvements

### Phase 5: Merge & Synthesize
- Combine individual proposals into coherent improvement plan
- Resolve interfaces and design tradeoffs
- Create implementation roadmap with realistic milestones

### Phase 6: Implementation
- Implement proposed components with clean interfaces
- Follow existing codebase conventions
- Include documentation

### Phase 7: Experiments
- Validate on appropriate benchmark datasets
- Measure relevant metrics (accuracy, throughput, etc.)
- Compare against current baselines

### Phase 8: Analysis
- Analyze results and identify remaining improvement opportunities
- Perform ablation studies to understand contributions
- Diagnose root causes for any underperformance
- **Determine if another iteration is needed for further gains**

## 🔄 ITERATION POLICY
After analysis, if significant improvement opportunities are identified:
- Focus next iteration on the highest-leverage bottleneck
- Apply targeted research to that specific area
- Validate that improvements are meaningful and practical

## ✅ SUCCESS CRITERIA
- Achieve measurable improvements over baselines
- Each phase produces documented artifacts
- Proposals are grounded in research evidence
- Implementations are validated experimentally
- Iterations are justified by data, not assumptions
