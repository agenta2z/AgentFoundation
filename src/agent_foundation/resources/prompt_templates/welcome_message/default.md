## 👋 Welcome to MRS RankEvolve!

I'm your AI research partner for evolving Meta's ranking models. Together we'll investigate, research, propose, implement, and iterate — all powered by parallel AI agents.

### 🚀 How it works

- **🔧 Setup** — Tell me your target codebase and pick an evolution strategy:
  - 💡 Paradigm-Shifting — discover breakthrough architectures
  - 📊 Systematic — structured analysis of bottlenecks for meaningful gains
  - 🎯 Targeted — solve specific problems (training instability, latency, etc.)
  - 🔭 Exploratory — survey the landscape and spot opportunity areas
  - ⚡ Efficiency — optimize memory, throughput, and inference latency

- **🔍 Investigate** — I'll analyze your codebase to map out the architecture, data flow, model structure, and extension points.

- **📚 Research & Propose** — I'll break your goal into sub-queries, run deep research in parallel across multiple AI agents, and synthesize the findings into a unified design proposal.

- **⚙️ Implement & Analyze** — I'll plan the changes, implement them, run experiments, and analyze results to find what's working and what needs more work.

- **🔄 Evolve** — Based on the analysis, we loop back to any earlier phase. Each iteration builds on prior results and workspace artifacts, continuously refining the approach.

### 💬 Getting started

Just type a message to chat, or use one of these commands:
- `/understand-codebase` — Investigate a codebase
- `/research-propose` — Run deep research with parallel AI agents
- `/task` — Execute a full research-implement-evaluate cycle
- `/help` — See all available commands
