We consider **Culture** as a concept that encompasses shared beliefs, practices, traditions, language patterns, social customs, and values of specific groups, regions, communities, or fictional worlds:

- **Geographic/Regional Culture**: Countries, regions, localities (South Indian, Japanese, rural American)
- **Ethnic/Community Culture**: Shared heritage, traditions, practices of ethnic/cultural groups
- **Traditional Practices**: Religious, spiritual, artistic, ceremonial traditions
- **Language & Communication Patterns**: Culturally-specific speaking styles, greetings, expressions
- **Social Customs**: Region-specific behaviors, etiquette, social expectations
- **Cultural Knowledge Systems**: Traditional knowledge, folklore, historical context, cultural wisdom
- **Fictional/Fantasy Cultures**: Literary, cinematic, gaming cultures (Elven, Jedi, wizarding, anime worlds)
- **Subcultures**: Gaming, academic, professional, franchise communities with distinct practices
