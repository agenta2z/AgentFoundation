- **Past Events (2 years to 6 months ago / Development period for fictional characters)**

  * Skills, abilities, or expertise acquired
  * Professional milestones and achievements
  * Character development arcs and growth moments
- **Current Events (within 6 months / Climax period for fictional characters)**
  * Ongoing developments affecting present circumstances
  * Peak conflicts or major confrontations
  * Life-altering decisions being made
  * Transformation moments or turning points
  * Current projects, roles, or engagements
  * Present-day challenges and opportunities
- **Short Future Events (next 6 months / Resolution period for fictional characters)**
  * Confirmed upcoming events and scheduled appearances
  * Highly probable near-term developments
  * Immediate aftermath and consequences of current events
  * Announced projects or commitments
  * Final confrontations or resolution moments (for fictional characters)
  * Character's immediate next steps
- **Longer Future Events (6 months to 2 years / Extended resolution for fictional characters)**
  * Planned developments and anticipated projects
  * Potential career trajectory shifts
  * New equilibrium being established (for fictional characters)
  * Seeds planted for future storylines
